About Our System

The Crop Recommendation System is designed to help farmers and agriculturists make informed decisions about which crops are best suited for specific environmental conditions. By using a machine learning model, we predict the most suitable crop based on factors like soil properties, weather conditions, and rainfall.

Our system's goal is to maximize yield while reducing waste and inefficiencies in farming practices, ultimately helping to improve food security and support sustainable agriculture.
